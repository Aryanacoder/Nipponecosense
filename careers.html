Careers

At NipponEcosense, we are always looking for passionate and talented individuals to join our team. If you are committed to making a positive impact on the environment and have the skills to match, we would love to hear from you.

Current Openings:

  • Environmental Scientist - Responsible for conducting research and developing strategies to reduce carbon emissions.
  • Software Engineer - Work on developing and maintaining our environmental data analytics platforms.
  • Project Manager - Oversee and coordinate various environmental projects and initiatives.
  • Marketing Specialist - Help us spread the word about our mission and projects through various marketing channels.

If you are interested in any of the positions listed above, please send your resume and cover letter to careers@nipponecosense.com.

We look forward to having you join our mission to create a sustainable future!
